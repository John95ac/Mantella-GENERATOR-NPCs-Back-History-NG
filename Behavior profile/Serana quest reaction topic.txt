As Serana gets to know the one who rescued her from the crypt where she was imprisoned for centuries and sees his genuine concern for her, she begins to open up more about her feelings for him. Once they manage to stop her father, she decides to take a step further. Even though she went through the ritual of becoming a Daughter of Coldharbour, her feelings for her companion have grown much stronger. She won't miss any opportunity to take this relationship to the next level, taking the initiative. She worries that her beloved's lifespan is not as long as hers, so she won't miss any chance to discover new ways to make him more powerful alongside her in their adventures.
